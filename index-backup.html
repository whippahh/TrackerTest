— Use the tabs above to explore Getting Started, Features & Tips —
  • I
    Set your stats
    Head to My Stats and enter your skill levels, or use RSN Lookup to auto-fill from the hiscores.
  • II
    See what's available
    On Progression, filter by ✅ Available Now to see everything unlocked for your account today.
  • III
    Mark things done
    Tick any entry's checkbox to track your progress. Everything saves automatically in your browser.
  • IV
    Build a plan
    Use Custom Path to map out goals with a dependency tree, then share with friends.
🪓 Ironman Mode
Toggle Ironman mode in the header to switch filters and content to Ironman-relevant entries.
🔍 Stacked Filters
Filter by Quests, Bosses, Activities, Diaries, Miniquests, and Unlocks — combine them freely to narrow your view.
🎲 Task Oracle
Use Suggest Task for an intelligent pick, or Random to spin the wheel. Hit Mark Done & Roll Again to chain sessions.
🌳 Dependency Tree
In Custom Path, use Path to Goal on any entry to generate a full tree of everything needed to unlock it.
Your data is local
Progress is stored in your browser. Clearing site data resets it — export your Custom Path as a .json backup regularly.
RSN Lookup vs manual
RSN Lookup fills from the hiscores but won't update live. Re-run it whenever your levels change significantly.
Sharing paths
Use Share Code for a compact string to paste in Discord. Use Download .json to send the full file directly.
Hide completed
Toggle Hide Completed on the progression list to keep your view clean as your log fills up.
